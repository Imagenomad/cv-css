A little about me

I have degrees in optometry and medicine and a PhD in visual physiology. Since retiring from medical practice, I have transitioned to web development through intensive participation in MOOCs such as those provided by Udemy and The App Brewery along with studying with the Open University. In my spare time, I play ukulele, guitar and bass, and sequence and produce my own music. I am also a photographer with a large, widely-published stock library.

My Skills

Web Development

I am a full stack web developer using HTML, CSS and Javascript to code front-end web content and MongoDB, React, Angular, node.js... to code the back-end. My photographic and musical creative pursuits inform my web design abilities.

Technical & Scientific Writing

I have authored or co-authored over 20 scientific papers in peer-reviewed journals and served on the editorial board of a journal focused on continuing education for optometrists. This experience has given me the skills to be a proficient technical and scientific writer, able to work to deadlines and word limits.
My broad education has also given me a grasp of a wide range of subjects, allowing me to write clearly and knowledgeably for a range of audiences, from the general public to experts.
